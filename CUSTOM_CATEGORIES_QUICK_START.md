## ğŸ“± How to Create Your First Custom Category

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Open Settings**
   - Tap Settings tab at bottom
   
2. **Go to Budget Preferences**
   - Tap "Budget Preferences" row
   - (Green dollar sign icon)

3. **Add Custom Category**
   - Scroll to "Custom Categories" section
   - Tap "+ Add Custom Category" button

4. **Fill in Details**
   - **Name:** e.g., "Coffee", "Gym", "Gaming"
   - **Budget:** Monthly amount (e.g., 150)
   - **Icon:** Tap to choose from icon picker
   - **Color:** Select theme color

5. **Save**
   - Tap "Add Category" button
   - Category instantly appears in Tracker
