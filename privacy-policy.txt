Privacy Policy for Chrema

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

INTRODUCTION
Chrema ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We collect and process the following information:
- Email address (for authentication)
- Financial information you provide (income, expenses, budget goals)
- Usage data (how you interact with the app)

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
Your information is used to:
- Provide personalized budget recommendations
- Track your expenses and savings goals
- Improve our services
- Communicate important updates

DATA STORAGE
- All data is stored securely using Firebase/Google Cloud
- We use industry-standard encryption
- Your financial data is never shared with third parties for marketing

YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of communications

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
We use:
- Google Firebase (authentication and data storage)
- Anthropic Claude AI (budget recommendations - data is not stored)

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Chrema is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
